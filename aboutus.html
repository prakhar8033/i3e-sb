ABOUT IEEE

The IEEE student branch, ever since its establishment has been working consistently to keep the members abreast with cutting edge technology and also it has always dedicated towards the enhancement of the personalities of those associated with it.

In pursuit of excellence and thereby making each one competent for the professional world, the student branch keeps its members engaged throughout the year by a string of events. The enthusiasm of being an IEEE member reaches its peak with techSRIJAN, a national level technical festival.

ABOUT MMMUT

IEEE and Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, Gorakhpur, one of the premier non-affiliating, teaching and research University of U.P. State Govt., presents an organization fostering the innovative technocrats of the country, in the form of IEEE student branch. Founded in 1988 with the sincere efforts of the faculty as well the students, it is one of the one thousand six hundred students branches the world over. A branch started with a meager number of members today enjoys a membership near to two hundred.

In IEEE MMMUT branch we aim to introduce the IEEE to the students in our university and give them a general Idea of what is the IEEE and what it offers for you and create a connection between them and the best community out there to reach to there fellow knowledge seekers also we hold different kind of events ,workshops , training , volunteer worksâ€¦etc all for the purpose of a better society and better world.
